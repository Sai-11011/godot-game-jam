# Titan's Wake

A fast-paced arena survival game where you, the **Relic Warrior**, must harvest power from **Titan Shards** to evolve your weapon. Will you survive the lethal expanding **Resonance Zone** and defeat the ancient Titan that awakens after 5 minutes? 

Developed for the Godot Game Jam.

## 🎯 High Concept

A fast-paced arena survival game where the player must harvest power from Titan Shards to evolve their weapon, all while a lethal Resonance Zone expands, leading to a final climatic battle against an ancient Titan.

## ⚔️ Mechanics & Progression
- **Crimson Shards (Red):** Focuses on Offense. Grants +5% Base Attack Damage.
- **Azure Shards (Blue):** Focuses on Agility. Grants +7% Movement Speed.
- **Emerald Shards (Green):** Focuses on Survivability. Grants +15 Max Health and an instant minor heal.
- **The Prime Heart:** Appears at the 4:00 mark. Grants a 50% boost to all current stats and Infinite Charges for the Heavy Attack.

## 👾 Enemies
- **The Shard-Seeker:** Fast, low-HP scavengers that eat uncollected shards.
- **The Resonance Sentinel:** Slow, high-HP tanks with a Repulsion Aura.
- **The Void Leech:** Ranged disruptors that slow the player's movement.

## 🚀 Setup & Running
1. Clone the repository.
2. Open Godot Engine.
3. Import the `project.godot` file.
4. Run the main scene!
